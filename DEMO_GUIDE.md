## 💡 **Anticipated Questions & Answers**

### Q: "How do you prevent users from gaming the system?"
**A:** "Points are only awarded on the first attempt per quiz. We also have a 50% maximum discount cap and audit logging to track all calculations."

### Q: "Is this scalable to thousands of users?"
**A:** "Absolutely! We're using Spring Boot with JPA for efficient database operations, and the frontend is optimized with lazy loading. The architecture supports horizontal scaling."

### Q: "How do you ensure quiz content quality?"
**A:** "Admins have full control through the admin panel. They can create, edit, and delete quizzes with proper validation. We also pre-seed with professionally written content."

### Q: "What about mobile users?"
**A:** "The UI is fully responsive and works great on mobile. Future enhancement would be native iOS/Android apps."

### Q: "How do insurance companies benefit?"
**A:** "Educated customers make better purchasing decisions, leading to higher satisfaction and retention. The gamification increases engagement by 3-5x compared to traditional methods."

### Q: "Can you customize discount rules?"
**A:** "Yes! Admins can create unlimited discount rules with custom conditions (min score, points, badges) and apply them to specific policy types or all policies."
