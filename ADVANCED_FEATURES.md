# 🎮 IQsure - Advanced Gamification Features

## 🌟 **Impressive Features That Set Us Apart**

### 1. **🏆 Achievement System**
- **8 Unique Achievements** to unlock
- **Progressive Difficulty** - From "First Steps" to "Badge Collector"
- **Visual Progress Tracking** - See locked vs unlocked achievements
- **Point Rewards** - Each achievement grants bonus points
- **Real-time Updates** - Achievements unlock instantly

**Achievements Include:**
- 🎓 First Steps (10 pts) - Complete your first quiz
- 💯 Perfect Score (50 pts) - Score 100% on any quiz
- 🔥 On Fire (30 pts) - Complete 3 quizzes in one day
- 📚 Knowledge Seeker (75 pts) - Complete all 3 quiz categories
- 💎 Premium Hunter (100 pts) - Purchase your first policy
- 🎯 Sharpshooter (150 pts) - Score 80%+ on 5 quizzes
- 👑 Top 10 (200 pts) - Reach top 10 on leaderboard
- 🌟 Badge Collector (250 pts) - Unlock all badges

- **5-Minute Countdown** for each quiz
- **Dynamic Speed Bonuses:**
  - Under 2 minutes = 50% bonus points
  - Under 3 minutes = 25% bonus points
  - Under 4 minutes = 10% bonus points
- **Color-Coded Timer** - Green → Yellow → Red as time runs out
- **Auto-Submit** - Quiz submits automatically when time expires
- **Animated Bonus Display** - Pulsing speed bonus indicator

### 3. **⚡ Level System**
- **Progressive Leveling** - Level up every 100 points
- **Visual Progress Bar** - See exactly how close you are to next level
- **Level Display** - Current level shown on achievements page
- **Unlimited Levels** - No cap on how high you can go

### 4. **💰 Savings Calculator**
- **Total Savings Tracker** - See cumulative savings from all policies
- **Potential Savings** - Calculate what you could save on remaining policies
- **Visual Comparison** - Before/After pricing with arrows
- **Savings Breakdown** - Individual policy savings displayed
- **Actionable Tips** - 3 tips to maximize savings

### 5. **📊 Completion Rate Tracking**
